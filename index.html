About

Stephanie is a VR Designer and Developer focused on promoting code literacy through art and simulation. Her goals are learning the technical aspects of digital spaces through a critical and creative approach, making all attempts to share the knowledge through online tutorials, in person workshops, or simple conversations. She has taught programming through Art and WebVR at after school camps, conferences, and at the MIT Media Lab.
